<p><p>Thin film deposition services encompass two primary market types: chemical deposition and physical deposition. Chemical deposition involves processes such as chemical vapor deposition (CVD) and atomic layer deposition (ALD), where chemical reactions create thin films on substrates. Physical deposition includes methods like sputtering and evaporation, relying on physical processes to deposit material onto surfaces. Both types are essential in industries like electronics, optics, and coatings, enabling the production of advanced materials with precise thickness and properties for various applications.</p></p>
