<p><p>The flame retardant material market is primarily categorized into two types: organic and inorganic flame retardant materials. Organic flame retardants, often derived from carbon-based compounds, are known for their effectiveness in enhancing flexibility and processing characteristics in various applications, such as plastics and textiles. In contrast, inorganic flame retardants, typically made from mineral-based compounds, offer high thermal stability and are less prone to leaching, making them suitable for construction materials and electronics. Both types play crucial roles in improving fire safety across multiple industries.</p></p>
