<p><p>The high-resolution inspection system market comprises primarily two types: optical inspection systems and X-ray inspection systems. Optical inspection systems utilize cameras and advanced imaging technologies to detect surface defects and irregularities in materials, facilitating quality control in manufacturing. In contrast, X-ray inspection systems employ X-ray imaging to uncover internal flaws or structural anomalies without damaging the object. Both systems are crucial for ensuring product integrity, enhancing safety, and maintaining high standards in various industries, including electronics, automotive, and aerospace.</p></p>
