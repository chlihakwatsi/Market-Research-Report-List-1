<p><p>Table tennis blades come in various handle types, each catering to different playing styles. The FL (Flared) handle is popular for its ergonomic design, providing a comfortable grip for fast exchanges. The ST (Straight) handle offers a simple, uniform grip preferred by some players for its versatility. The AN (Anatomic) handle is contoured to fit the hand, enhancing control. The Penhold-CS handle is designed for players using the penhold grip, facilitating quick wrist movements. Other handles include unique designs catering to specific preferences or techniques.</p></p>
