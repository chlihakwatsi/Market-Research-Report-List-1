<p><strong>The Proportional Counter Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Gas-flow Proportional Counter</li><li>Sealed Tubes Proportional Counter</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Proportional counters are radiation detection devices that operate based on gas ionization. In gas-flow proportional counters, gas is continuously circulated, allowing for effective detection of ionizing radiation over extended periods. These counters are advantageous for monitoring low radiation levels. Sealed tube proportional counters, on the other hand, contain a fixed volume of gas within a sealed chamber, providing a stable environment for measuring radiation. Both types are essential in various applications, including environmental monitoring, medical imaging, and safety assessment.</p></p>
