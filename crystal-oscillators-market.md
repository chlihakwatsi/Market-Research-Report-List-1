<p><p>The crystal oscillators market is categorized into types such as surface mount and thru-hole, based on their mounting technique. Surface mount oscillators are compact and suitable for automated assembly, while thru-hole types offer durability in demanding environments. Additionally, AT cut, BT cut, and SC cut refer to the crystal cuts that determine frequency stability and temperature characteristics. AT cut is favored for consumer electronics, BT cut is used in high-frequency applications, and SC cut provides excellent temperature stability. Other types include specialty crystals for unique applications.</p></p>
