<p><ul><li>Lifting</li><li>Rotary Opening</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Double Flap Isolation Valve market encompasses two main types: lifting and rotary opening valves. Lifting valves operate by raising the flaps to allow fluid flow, ideal for high-pressure applications, providing a tight seal and preventing backflow. Rotary opening valves, on the other hand, utilize a rotating mechanism to open or close the flaps, offering quicker operation and compact design. Both types are essential for controlling flow in industrial systems, enhancing efficiency and reliability in various applications.</p></p>
