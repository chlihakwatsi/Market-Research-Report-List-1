<p><p>The Commercial Acousto-Optic Tunable Filters (AOTFs) market is divided into two main types: collinear and noncollinear AOTFs. Collinear AOTFs align the acoustic and optical beams parallel, allowing for efficient light modulation and high throughput, making them ideal for applications requiring precise tuning. Noncollinear AOTFs, on the other hand, utilize beams angled relative to each other, providing broader wavelength tuning range and flexibility, suitable for diverse spectroscopic and imaging applications. Both types cater to distinct industrial and scientific needs.</p></p>
