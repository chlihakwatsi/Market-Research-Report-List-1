<p><strong>The FACTS Devices Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>SVC</li><li>SVG</li></ul></p>
<p><p>FACTS (Flexible AC Transmission Systems) devices enhance the efficiency and stability of power transmission. Within this market, Static Var Compensators (SVC) provide dynamic voltage support and reactive power compensation, improving system reliability. In contrast, Static Synchronous Generators (SVG) offer advanced voltage stabilization and active/reactive power management, allowing for faster response times. Both SVC and SVG are crucial for modern grids, supporting renewable energy integration and enhancing the overall performance of electrical systems. Their roles are vital in ensuring grid resilience.</p></p>
