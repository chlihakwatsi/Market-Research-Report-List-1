<p><p>Cold cast elastomers are versatile materials used in various applications, primarily categorized into two types: two-component and three-component systems. Two-component systems consist of a resin and a hardener, offering ease of use and flexibility in production. Three-component systems introduce an additional component, often a curing agent, enhancing properties like strength and durability. Both types cater to diverse industrial needs, enabling tailored solutions for specific applications, while maintaining the advantages of cold casting, such as low processing temperatures and efficient production methods.</p></p>
