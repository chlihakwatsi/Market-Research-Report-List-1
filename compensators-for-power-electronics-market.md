<p><ul><li>Static Synchronous Series Compensator (SSSC)</li><li>Thyristor-Switched Series Capacitor (TSSC)</li><li>Thyristor-Switched Series Reactor (TSSR)</li><li>Thyristor-Controlled Series Capacitor (TCSC)</li><li>Thyristor-Controlled Series Reactor (TCSR)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Compensators for power electronics enhance power system stability and efficiency through various technology types. The Static Synchronous Series Compensator (SSSC) uses voltage source converters for dynamic control of power flow. Thyristor-Switched Series Capacitors (TSSC) and Thyristor-Switched Series Reactors (TSSR) manage power fluctuations by switching capacitors or reactors on and off. Thyristor-Controlled Series Capacitors (TCSC) and Thyristor-Controlled Series Reactors (TCSR) provide continuous control by varying reactance, optimizing load management and voltage stability in electrical networks.</p></p>
