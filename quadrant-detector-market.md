<p><p>The Quadrant Detector market is divided into two main types: 2-quadrant detectors and 4-quadrant detectors. 2-quadrant detectors primarily measure signals in two quadrants, making them suitable for basic applications where directionality is not critical. In contrast, 4-quadrant detectors offer more advanced functionality by capturing signals in all four quadrants, enabling precise measurements of both intensity and direction. This versatility finds applications in robotics, optical systems, and advanced imaging technologies, driving growth in the market.</p></p>
