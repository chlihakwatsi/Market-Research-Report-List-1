<p><strong>The Waste-to-Energy Technologies Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Thermal Technologies</li><li>Biochemical Reactions</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Waste-to-Energy technologies convert waste materials into usable energy, with two primary market types: thermal technologies and biochemical reactions. Thermal technologies include processes like incineration and gasification, where heat is used to generate electricity or produce fuel from waste. In contrast, biochemical reactions involve the decomposition of organic waste through anaerobic digestion or fermentation, producing biogas and biofuels. Both approaches aim to reduce landfill waste while generating renewable energy, contributing to sustainability and energy recovery efforts.</p></p>
