<p><p>The high-gluten flour market comprises two primary types: machine milled flour and stone ground flour. Machine milled flour is produced using modern milling techniques, resulting in a fine, uniform texture ideal for commercial baking applications like bread and pizza dough. In contrast, stone ground flour is crafted through traditional methods using millstones, preserving more bran and germ, which can enhance flavor and nutritional value. Both types cater to different consumer preferences and baking requirements within the market.</p></p>
