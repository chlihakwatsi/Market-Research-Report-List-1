<p><p>The Low Power CW Fiber Laser market is categorized into two main types: single mode lasers and multimode lasers. Single mode lasers deliver a focused, coherent beam with minimal divergence, making them ideal for precision applications like medical procedures and telecommunications. In contrast, multimode lasers emit light through multiple paths, providing higher power output and broader beam profiles, suitable for industrial applications such as material processing and laser engraving. Both types serve distinct needs across various industries, expanding their market potential.</p></p>
