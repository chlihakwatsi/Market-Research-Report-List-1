<p><p>The 2-Channel Wireless Audio System market encompasses various types of transmission technologies. RF (Radio Frequency) systems offer long-range communication and are ideal for large areas, enabling users to enjoy audio without line-of-sight limitations. IR (Infrared) systems provide high-quality sound but require direct visibility between transmitter and receiver, making them suitable for close-range applications. Bluetooth technology is popular for its convenience and compatibility with mobile devices, allowing streaming from smartphones and tablets. Other technologies include Wi-Fi and proprietary systems that cater to specific needs.</p></p>
