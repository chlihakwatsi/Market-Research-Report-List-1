<p><p>The semiconductor grade hydrochloric acid market is categorized into four types: SL Grade (Standard Level), UL Grade (Ultra Level), VL Grade (Very Low Level), and EL Grade (Electronic Level). SL Grade meets basic purity requirements for general semiconductor applications. UL Grade offers higher purity suitable for critical processes, while VL Grade ensures even lower impurity levels for advanced manufacturing needs. EL Grade provides the highest purity, essential for the most sensitive semiconductor production processes, minimizing contamination risks.</p></p>
