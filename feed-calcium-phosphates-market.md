<p><p>The feed calcium phosphates market comprises various types essential for animal nutrition. Monocalcium Phosphate (MCP) is primarily used for poultry and swine, providing a readily available source of phosphorus. Dicalcium Phosphate (DCP) offers a balanced calcium to phosphorus ratio, suitable for ruminants. Mono-Dicalcium Phosphate (MDCP) combines qualities of both MCP and DCP, enhancing absorption. Tricalcium Phosphate (TCP) serves as a calcium source and is often used in supplements. Together, these products support growth, bone development, and overall animal health.</p></p>
