<p><p>The non-woven adhesive market features various types, including SBC-based and APAO-based adhesives. SBC-based adhesives, derived from styrene-butadiene copolymers, offer excellent elasticity and are widely used in applications requiring flexibility, such as hygiene products. Conversely, APAO-based adhesives, made from amorphous polyalphaolefins, provide superior thermal stability and adhesion strength, making them suitable for applications such as automotive and construction. Both types are integral to enhancing product performance in the non-woven sector, catering to diverse industrial needs.</p></p>
