<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The RFID Labels Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>HF RFID</li><li>UHF RFID</li><li>LF RFID</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The RFID labels market is classified into three primary types: HF RFID, UHF RFID, and LF RFID. HF RFID operates at 13.56 MHz, ideal for short-range applications like access control and smart cards. UHF RFID, ranging from 860 to 960 MHz, offers longer read distances, making it suitable for inventory tracking and supply chain management. LF RFID functions at 125-134 kHz, often used for animal identification and keyless entry systems. Each type serves distinct applications based on frequency, range, and functionality.</p></p>
