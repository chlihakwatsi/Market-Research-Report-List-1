<p><p>The Spring Brake Chamber market comprises primarily two types: Diaphragm and Piston types. Diaphragm type brake chambers utilize a flexible diaphragm to apply braking force, making them simpler and more reliable for heavy-duty applications. In contrast, Piston type chambers employ a piston mechanism, offering higher force output and efficiency, often suited for specialized uses. Both types are crucial for commercial vehicles, ensuring safety and effective braking performance, each catering to distinct operational requirements and industry preferences.</p></p>
