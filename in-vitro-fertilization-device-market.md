<p><p>The In Vitro Fertilization (IVF) device market comprises several techniques, including Frozen Embryo Transfer (FET), Donor Egg IVF, and Fresh Embryo Transfer. FET involves using previously frozen embryos, enhancing flexibility and timing in treatment. Donor Egg IVF utilizes eggs from a donor, addressing fertility issues related to egg quality. Fresh Embryo Transfer involves immediate transplantation of embryos created during the current cycle, maximizing the chances of conception. Each technique caters to specific fertility needs and preferences.</p></p>
