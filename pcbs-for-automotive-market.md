<p><p>PCBs for the automotive market encompass several types, each serving specific functions. HDI PCBs (High-Density Interconnect) enable compact designs for advanced electronics, while High-Frequency PCBs support high-speed communication systems essential for modern vehicles. FPC PCBs (Flexible Printed Circuits) provide adaptability in tight spaces, ideal for dynamic applications. Multilayer PCBs allow complex circuitry which is crucial for sophisticated vehicle systems. Other types include rigid and aluminum PCBs, catering to various automotive needs, enhancing performance and reliability in vehicles.</p></p>
