<p><strong>The Femoral Cannula Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Unipolar</li><li>Bipolar</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The femoral cannula market is categorized into unipolar and bipolar types, reflecting their design and functionality. </p><p>Unipolar cannulas feature a single lumen, allowing for straightforward drainage or infusion, and are often used in simpler procedures. In contrast, bipolar cannulas have dual lumens, enabling simultaneous drainage and infusion, which enhances procedural efficiency and patient outcomes. The choice between these types depends on the specific clinical requirements, such as the complexity of the procedure and the desired control over fluid management.</p></p>
