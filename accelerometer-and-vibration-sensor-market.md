<p><p>The accelerometer and vibration sensor market comprises two main types: AC-type and DC-type sensors. AC-type accelerometers measure dynamic acceleration and are suited for applications involving fast-moving systems, detecting vibrations and oscillations in real time. In contrast, DC-type accelerometers can measure both static and dynamic acceleration, making them ideal for monitoring tilt and orientation. Each type serves distinct applications, with AC-type sensors often used in automotive and aerospace industries, while DC-type sensors find use in consumer electronics and structural monitoring.</p></p>
