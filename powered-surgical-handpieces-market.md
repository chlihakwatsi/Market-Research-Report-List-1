<p><p>The powered surgical handpieces market is categorized into three primary types: electric-powered, battery-powered, and pneumatic-powered devices. Electric-powered handpieces offer consistent power and speed, making them suitable for various surgical procedures. Battery-powered handpieces provide portability and eliminate the need for cords, enhancing maneuverability in the operating room. Pneumatic-powered handpieces use compressed air, delivering high torque and lightweight design, often preferred in specific surgical applications. Each type caters to diverse surgical requirements and enhances operational efficiency.</p></p>
